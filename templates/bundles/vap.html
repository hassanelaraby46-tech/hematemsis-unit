VAP Prevention Bundle (Evidence-Based Practice)

  • 1. Head of Bed (HOB) Elevation: Keep the head of the bed elevated between 30 to 45 degrees for adults (and 15-30 degrees for pediatric patients) to prevent aspiration.
  • 2. Daily Sedation Vacation & Spontaneous Breathing Trials (SBT): Assess daily if the patient is ready for weaning. Reducing sedation promotes faster extubation.
  • 3. Oral Care with Chlorhexidine: Perform regular oral hygiene using 0.2% Chlorhexidine mouthwash to reduce bacterial colonization.
  • 4. Stress Ulcer Prophylaxis: Implement pharmacological prophylaxis to prevent stress-related gastrointestinal bleeding.
  • 5. Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT) Prophylaxis: Apply appropriate mechanical or pharmacological DVT prophylaxis for immobile ventilated patients.
  • 6. Subglottic Secretion Drainage: Use an endotracheal tube with a suction lumen to drain secretions above the cuff.
